Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Flight Vault membership.

How do you find these deals?

+

I use a combination of advanced search techniques, industry connections, and proprietary tools to spot pricing errors, hidden deals, and flash sales that most people miss. I monitor hundreds of routes daily to find the best flight opportunities for our members.

Are these deals legitimate?

+

Absolutely. All deals are for flights with reputable airlines and are booked directly through the airline's website or trusted partners. These are real tickets with the same guarantees and protections as any standard booking.

What's included in my vault access?

+

Your one-time payment gives you lifetime access to the Flight Vault, which includes all current deals, access to future deals as they're discovered, personalized alert settings, and our members-only travel resources. You'll receive weekly deal updates via email and can access the vault anytime.

How quickly do I need to book deals?

+

Error fares and flash sales can disappear quickly, sometimes within hours. We recommend booking as soon as you see a deal you like. However, many deals last several days, giving you time to coordinate with travel companions.

What if I can't find a deal that works for me?

+

If you don't find a deal that saves you money within 30 days of joining, we offer a full refund. However, with dozens of new deals each month covering destinations worldwide, most members find valuable deals quickly that exceed the cost of membership.

Can I request deals for specific destinations?

+

Yes! Members can set up alerts for specific destinations, and I also take requests for destinations to prioritize in my searches. While I can't guarantee deals to every destination, I do my best to find options that work for all members.
