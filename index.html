Flight deals are subject to availability and can sell out quickly. We recommend booking as soon as you find a deal that interests you. We regularly update our deals to remove any that are no longer available.

Once you've paid the one-time fee, you'll have access to the Flight Vault for as long as it exists. You'll receive all future updates and new deals at no additional cost.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied? +

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with the Flight Vault, contact us within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund.

How often are new deals added? +

We add new deals weekly, with major updates happening at least once a month. The best deals are typically added as soon as they become available.

Are these deals available from airports outside of London? +

Currently, most of our deals are from London airports. However, we're continuously expanding to include more departure cities across the UK and Europe.
