Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I've got answers. Here are some of the most common questions I receive.

How do you find these deals? +

I use a combination of advanced search techniques, industry connections, and proprietary tools to spot pricing errors, hidden deals, and flash sales that most people miss. I spend hours every day searching for the best flight deals so you don't have to.

Are these deals trustworthy? +

Absolutely! All deals are for flights with reputable airlines and are booked directly through the airline's website or trusted partners. These are real tickets with the same guarantees and protections as any standard booking.

What's included in my vault access? +

Your one-time payment gives you lifetime access to the Flight Vault, which includes all current deals, access to future deals as they're discovered, destination guides, and my personal travel recommendations. You can also opt-in to email alerts for specific destinations.

How quickly do I need to book deals? +

Error fares and flash sales can disappear quickly, sometimes within hours. I recommend booking as soon as you see a deal you like. However, many deals last several days, giving you time to coordinate with travel companions.

What if I can't find a deal that works for me? +

If you don't find a deal that saves you money within 30 days of joining, I offer a full refund. However, with dozens of new deals each month covering destinations worldwide, most members find valuable deals quickly.

Can I request deals for specific destinations? +

Yes! Members can set up alerts for specific destinations, and I also take requests for destinations to prioritize in my searches. While I can't guarantee deals to every destination, I do my best to find options that work for my members.
